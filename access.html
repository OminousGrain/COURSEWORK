In designing the site accessibility for people who have issues such as colour-blindness is an important consideration. Ideally, we want our site to be accessible to the widest range of people possible. As such, stylistic choices can help or hinder this.


In designing the site I took this into account, by changing the contrast of paragraphs when hovered over, hopefully the stark contrast helps the visually impaired.


Another consideration could be including descriptive alternative text for images on the site, this helps those who are navigating our site using TTS (text-to-speech) devices.
